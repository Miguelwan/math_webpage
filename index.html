I am doctor in mathematics, currently a senior postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Mathematics of the University of Vienna, leader of the project Generalized Baire spaces, structures and combinatorics .

My research is focus on Mathematical Logic, set theory and model theory. I am also interested in different applications of mathematics, and popular science.
